What is Satellite Communication?

  • Satellite communication transmits and receives information using satellites orbiting the Earth
  • It enables long-distance wireless communication for television, internet, and phone services
  • It connects ground stations by relaying signals, overcoming geographical barriers
  • Satellite communication is vital for communication networks, navigation, and space exploration

Satellite Launching Process

  • Rockets launch satellites into space
  • They ascend vertically at first
  • Boosters provide thrust initially
  • Used boosters are dropped to reduce weight
  • Lighter rockets move faster
  • At the correct altitude, satellites are released into orbit

How Satellites Work

  • Satellite communication consists of three main components
  • Uplink: transmitting signals from the ground to the satellite
  • Satellite transponder: amplifying and converting signals
  • Downlink: transmitting signals from the satellite to the ground
